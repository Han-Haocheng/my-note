# 克莱姆法则

定理6（克莱姆法则）：含有$n$个方程$n$个未知数的$n$元线性方程组$\begin{cases}a_{11}x_{1}+a_{12}x_{2}+\dots+a_{1n}x_{n}=b_{1} \\ a_{21}x_{1}+a_{22}x_{2}+\dots+a_{2n}x_{n}=b_{2} \\ \dots \\ a_{n1}x_{1}+a_{n2}x_{2}+\dots+a_{nn}x_{n}=b_{n}\end{cases}$，当系数行列式$D=\begin{vmatrix}a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1n} \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\ a_{n1} & a_{n 2} & \dots & a_{nn}\end{vmatrix}\neq 0$时，方程组有唯一解，且唯一解为$x_{1}=\frac{D_{1}}{D},x_{2}=\frac{D_{2}}{D},\dots,x_{n}=\frac{D_{n}}{D}$，其中，$D_{1}=\begin{vmatrix}b_{1} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1n} \\ b_{2} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\ b_{n} & a_{n 2} & \dots & a_{nn}\end{vmatrix},$$D_{2}=\begin{vmatrix}a_{11} & b_{1} & \dots & a_{1n} \\ a_{21} & b_{2} & \dots & a_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\ a_{n1} & b_{n } & \dots & a_{nn}\end{vmatrix},$$\dots$$,D_{n}=\begin{vmatrix}a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & b_{1} \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & b_{2} \\ \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\ a_{n1} & a_{n 2} & \dots & b_{n}\end{vmatrix}$，即行列式$D_{j}$是系数行列式$D$中第$j$列元素替换为方程右端的常数项所得


定理7：含有$n$个方程$n$个未知数的$n$元齐次线性方程组$\begin{cases}a_{11}x_{1}+a_{12}x_{2}+\dots+a_{1n}x_{n}=0 \\ a_{21}x_{1}+a_{22}x_{2}+\dots+a_{2n}x_{n}=0 \\ \dots \\ a_{n1}x_{1}+a_{n2}x_{2}+\dots+a_{nn}x_{n}=0\end{cases}$，当系数行列式$D=\begin{vmatrix}a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1n} \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\ a_{n1} & a_{n 2} & \dots & a_{nn}\end{vmatrix}\neq 0$时，次方程组只有0解


- 即若此齐次线性方程组有非零解，则其系数行列式$D=0$.
- 当系数行列式$D=0$时，此齐次线性方程组必有非零解
	- 即上述齐次线性方程组有非零解的充分必要条件是系数行列式$D=0$
	- 只有零解的充分必要条件是系数行列式$D\neq0$.

## 例题

例题1：求线性方程组$\begin{cases}2x_{1}-x_{2}+x_{3} & =0 \\ 3x_{1}+2x_{2}-5x_{3} & =1 \\ x_{1}+3x_{2}-2x_{3} & = 4\end{cases}$
- $D=\begin{vmatrix}2 & -1 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 & -5 \\ 1 & 3 & -2\end{vmatrix}=28\neq 0$
- $x_{1}= \frac{\begin{vmatrix}0 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & -5 \\ 4 & 3 & -2\end{vmatrix}}{\begin{vmatrix}2 & -1 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 & -5 \\ 1 & 3 & -2\end{vmatrix}}=\frac{13}{28}$
- $x_{1}= \frac{\begin{vmatrix}2 & 0 & 1 \\ 3 & 1 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 & -2\end{vmatrix}}{\begin{vmatrix}2 & -1 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 & -5 \\ 1 & 3 & -2\end{vmatrix}}=\frac{47}{28}$
- $x_{1}= \frac{\begin{vmatrix}2 & -1 & 0 \\ 3 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 & 4\end{vmatrix}}{\begin{vmatrix}2 & -1 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 & -5 \\ 1 & 3 & -2\end{vmatrix}}=\frac{21}{28}=\frac{3}{4}$



例题2：求解线性方程组$\begin{cases}x_{1}+x_{2}+x_{3}+x_{4}=1\\x_{1}+2x_{2}+3x_{3}-x_{4}=1\\x_{1}+4x_{2}+9x_{3}+x_{4}=1\\x_{1}+8x_{2}+27x_{3}-x_{4}=1\end{cases}$
- $D=\begin{vmatrix}1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 & -1 \\ 1 & 4 & 9 & 1 \\ 1 & 8 & 27 & -1\end{vmatrix}$
- 使用范德蒙德行列式：$=(2-1)(3-1)(-1-1)(3-2)(-1-2)(-1-3)$
- $=1\times2\times -2\times1\times-3\times-4=-48\neq 0$
- 使用克莱姆法则
- $D_{1}=-48,D_{2}=0,D_{3}=0,D_{4}=0$
- $x_{1}=1,x_{2}=0,x_{3}=0,x_{4}=0$


【例3】判断下列齐次线性方程组是否有非零解？  
(1) $\begin{cases} x_1 + x_2 - 2x_3 = 0 \\ 5x_1 - 2x_2 + 7x_3 = 0 \\ 2x_1 - 5x_2 + 4x_3 = 0 \end{cases}$；
- $D=\begin{vmatrix}1 & 1 & -2 \\ 5 & -2 & 7 \\ 2 & -5 & 4\end{vmatrix}=63\neq 0$，只有0解


(2) $\begin{cases} 2x_1 - 3x_2 + 4x_3 - 3x_4 = 0 \\ 3x_1 - x_2 + 11x_3 - 13x_4 = 0 \\ 4x_1 + 5x_2 - 7x_3 - 2x_4 = 0 \\ 13x_1 - 25x_2 + x_3 + 11x_4 = 0 \end{cases}$．

- $D=\begin{vmatrix}2 & -3 & 4 & -3 \\ 3 & -1  & 11 & -13 \\ 4 & 5 & -7 & -2 \\ 13 & 25 & 1 & 11 \end{vmatrix}=0$，有非零解
