例19 设U=f(x,y,z)有连续偏导，z=z(x,y)由xe^x - ye^y = z e^z 确定，求du。
解 U=f(x,y,z(x,y))$\begin{cases} U = f(x,y,z) \\x e^x - y e^y = z e^z \end{cases}$
对x求偏导：$\begin{cases} \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = f_1' \cdot 1 + f_2' \cdot 0 + f_3' \cdot \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \\e^x + x e^x = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} e^z + z e^z \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \end{cases}\Rightarrow \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = f_1' + f_3' \cdot \frac{e^x(1+x)}{e^z(1+z)}$
对y求偏导：$\begin{cases} \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = f_1' \cdot 0 + f_2' \cdot 1 + f_3' \cdot \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \\- e^y - y e^y = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} e^z + z e^z \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \end{cases}\Rightarrow \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = f_2' + f_3' \cdot \frac{-e^y(1+y)}{e^z(1+z)}$
