# 监督学习

**supervised learning/监督学习**： learn function between input and output label from data labeled with the right answers./从正确的标签中学习输入到输出的映射算法
- [[计算机科学/人工智能/回归算法]]、[[计算机科学/人工智能/分类算法]]


> [!example] 举例
> | input(X)              | output(Y)              | Application          |
> | --------------------- | ---------------------- | -------------------- |
> | email/电子邮件            | spam/垃圾?(0/1)          | spam filtering/垃圾过滤器 |
> | audio/音频              | text transcripts/文本翻译  | speech recognition   |
> | English/英语            | Spanish/西班牙语           | manchine translation |
> | ad/广告, user info/用户信息 | click/点击?(0/1)         | online advertising   |
> | image, radar info     | position of other cars | self-driving/自动驾驶    |
> | image of phone        | dent?(0/1)             | visual inspection    |
>
