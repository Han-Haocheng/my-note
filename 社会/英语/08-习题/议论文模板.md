# 议论文模板

- 针对某一个话题，说明结果，解释原因，提出解决方案

1. 积累万能利弊词汇
    - 利：enhance efficiency（提高效率）/ promote cultural exchange（促进文化交流）/ broaden horizons（拓宽视野）
    - 弊：cause addiction（导致成瘾）/ trigger health issues（引发健康问题）/ undermine interpersonal relationships（损害人际关系）
2. 熟记5种常用过渡词
    - 表对比：whereas, conversely
    - 表结果：consequently, accordingly
3. 注意句式多样性
    - 强调句：It is... that...
    - 条件句：Provided that..., ... will...


## 现象解释型

**现象解释型**：分析某一现象产生的原因、影响或背景

- **Introduction**
	- **Hook**：In the past decade, {现象} has become a focal point of global/social discussion/conference.
	- **Background**：With the advancement/improvement/development of {现象领域}, {现象表现} has emerged/appeared as both a challenge and an opportunity.
	- **Thesis Statement**：This phenomenon can be attributed to {原因1},{原因2} and {原因3}, while its implications deserve(is worth)  to be studied in depth(thorough examination).
- **Phenomenon Analysis**
	- **Primary Cause**: Foremost/Firstly, {原因1} serves as the cornerstone/basis of this trend/topic. {具体解释}. Take {案例} as an example: {说明}.
	- **Secondary causes**: Equally noteworthy is the role of {原因2}/It is other reason which lead to {现象} that is {原因2}.{因果逻辑分析}.
	- **effect**: The ripple effects of this phenomenon are twofold/complex(复杂的)/With this phenomenon comes is complex effects. Positively, {积极影响}. However, the darker/negative side/influence cannot be ignored: {消极影响}.
- **Conclusion**
	- **Restate Thesis**："In summary, the interplay/influence between {原因1} and {原因2} has fueled/promoted {现象名称}, creating both {积极总结} and {消极总结}.
	- **Recommendation/Suggestion**：To harness its benefits while mitigating risks, governments should {措施1}, whereas individuals ought to {措施2}.
	- **Forward-looking Statement**：Only through collaborative efforts can we transform this double-edged sword into a catalyst for sustainable development.

## 问题解决型

**解决问题型**：针对某一问题提出解决方案

- **Introduction**
	- **Hook**：Nowadays, {问题} has become a growing concern in {相关领域}.
		- With the rapid development of  {相关领域}, {问题} is becoming increasingly serious.
	- **Thesis Statement**: This essay/article will analyze the root causes of {问题核心} and propose feasible/workable solutions.
- **Causes Analysis and Solutions**
	- **Topic Sentence:** Three main factors contribute to this problem: 
	- **Primary Cause**: It is primary cause which can lead to {结果} that {原因1}. {解决方式}
	- **Secondary Cause**：And that's not all, {原因2} can also cause this problem to occur . {解决方式2}
	- **Root Cause**：But the root cause of {问题} is {原因3}. {解决方式}
- **Conclusion**
	- **Restatement**：In conclusion, while {问题} stems/comes from multiple/many sources/respects, it can be mitigated/resolve through {解决方案总结}.
	- **Forward-looking Statement**: If these measures/suggestions are implemented/achieved effectively, we can expect {积极展望} in the foreseeable/near future.

举例作文：环境污染、生活压力、就业困难

## 说明利弊型

**说明利弊型**：阐述某一事物或现象的利与弊

- **Introduction**
	- With the rapid development of {相关领域}, {对象} has become an integral part of modern life.
	- While it brings significant benefits, there are also potential drawbacks that deserve our attention. 
	- This essay will analyze both sides of the issue.
- **Advantage Body**
	- On the one hand, {对象} provides multiple advantages. 
	- Firstly, {优点1}. For example, {例证}
	- Secondly, {优点2}. which allows people to {好处}.
	- Most importantly, {核心优点} play a vital role in {相关领域}.
- **Disadvantage Body**
	- On the other hand, we cannot ignore its negative aspects.
	- The primary concern is {核心缺点}, which may lead to {后果}.
	- Additionally ,{缺点} could result in {影响}. 
	- Worst of all, {极端负面的影响} might occur if imporperly handled.
- **Conclusion**
	- In conclusion, while {对象} offers considerable benefits such as {优点概括}, its potential risks like {缺点概括} should not be overlooked.
	- Personally, I believe that {个人观点}.
	- Only through {建议} can we maximize its advantages and minimize the disadvantages.

## 观点论证型

- **Introduction**
	- Nowadays, the debate surrounding {主题} has sparked widespread discussion.
		- Nowadays, more people are discussing whether {主题} is good or not.
	- While some argue that {对立观点}, I firmly believe that {自身观点}.
		- In my opinion, {观点}.
		- I think this because {原因} and {原因}
	- This essay will demonstrate key reason supporting this standpoint/view/idea.
		- Let me explain my reasons.
- **Body**
	- Firstly, {核心论点1} plays a pivotal/important/main role in {相关领域}
	- For instance, {具体例子}
	- Statistics show that {数据支撑}, proving that {结论性陈述}.
	- Without {论点1}, {负面结果}.
		- First, {论点1}. For example, {例子}. This shows that {重复论点} is true. If we {照着做}, we can {好处}
	- Secondly,{核心论点2} creates profound ramifications/effects/results.
	- A notable case is {例子}
	- This phenomenon not only {直接效果} but also {连锁反应}.
	- Experts predict that {未来趋势}, which underscores the urgency/need/importance of this issue.
		- Second, {论点2}. If we don't {做某事}, {坏结果}. But if we {解决方案}, {结果}. So it's clear that {主题} is necessary .
- **Conclusion**
	- In conclusion, while {缺点}, the merits/benefits/advantages of {自己观点} significantly outweigh its drawbacks.(比较句)
	- To address/deal with/solve this challenge, we should {建议}
	- Only through these measures can we {理想}
		- **In conclusion**, I believe {主题} is important because {原因1} and {原因2}. I hope everyone can {建议行动}.
